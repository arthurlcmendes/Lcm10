Pasta Recipes

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients: Spaghetti, eggs, pecorino cheese, pancetta, black pepper, salt.

Instructions: Boil pasta until al dente. Cook pancetta until crispy. Mix eggs with cheese. Combine hot pasta with pancetta, remove from heat, stir in egg mixture, season with pepper.

Penne Alfredo

Ingredients: Penne pasta, butter, heavy cream, garlic, parmesan cheese, parsley.

Instructions: Cook pasta. Melt butter, sauté garlic, add cream, simmer, mix cheese. Combine with pasta and garnish.

Salad Recipes

Caesar Salad

Ingredients: Romaine lettuce, croutons, parmesan, anchovies, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil.

Instructions: Prepare dressing, toss lettuce with sauce, add croutons and cheese, finish with grilled chicken if desired.

Burger Recipes

Classic Beef Burger

Ingredients: Ground beef, salt, pepper, buns, lettuce, tomato, cheddar cheese.

Instructions: Shape patties, season, grill until cooked, toast buns, assemble with toppings.

Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients: Dark chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, flour, cocoa powder.

Instructions: Melt chocolate and butter, mix sugar and eggs, fold in flour, bake at 180°C for 25 minutes.
